Who am I?

I remeber the first video tape player I took apart, I was so fascinated by, what I now know to be capacitor, colorful cylinders. I was fascinated by how it works and took apart any old electronic I could. Video games and personal computers started to appear in my village and I would be glue to these contraptions. Looking back, it's pretty cool to know that STEM was always going to be the career path I would choose.
